2. App Tracking Transparency (iOS 14.5+)

When you first open NumHunt, iOS will ask whether you allow the app to track your activity across other companies’ apps and websites. This permission controls whether Google AdMob may use your device’s Advertising Identifier (IDFA) for personalized advertising.

  • Allow: AdMob may show personalized ads based on your interests.
  • Ask App Not to Track: AdMob will show non-personalized ads. App functionality is unchanged.

You can change this choice at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → NumHunt.

3. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy. Interstitial ads are shown after every two completed games.

Supabase

We use Supabase to store Daily Challenge results and power online multiplayer rooms. A random, anonymous identifier is generated on first launch and stored on your device and on Supabase servers. No email or password is associated with this identifier. Supabase’s privacy practices are described in the Supabase Privacy Policy.

Apple Game Center

Quick Match uses Apple’s Game Center framework for player matching. We do not store your Game Center ID on our servers. Apple’s data practices are governed by the Apple Privacy Policy.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Display your score and streak on the Daily Challenge leaderboard
  • Facilitate real-time online multiplayer game sessions
  • Show advertisements to keep the app free
  • Monitor SDK stability (crash reports via AdMob)

We do not sell your data to any third party.

5. Data Retention

  • Daily Challenge scores: Stored on Supabase indefinitely to maintain leaderboard history.
  • Online room data: Rooms are marked finished and cleaned up automatically after a session ends.
  • Ad data: Retained by Google per their own data retention policies.
  • Local game history: Stored on your device only. Deleted when you uninstall the app.

6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You may request deletion of all data associated with your anonymous Supabase account at any time. To do so, contact us at the email address below and include the phrase “Delete my NumHunt data.” We will process your request within 30 days.

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data (game history, settings, anonymous ID) from your device.

7. Children’s Privacy

NumHunt is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

All communication between the app and Supabase servers is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. We take reasonable measures to protect the information we hold, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
