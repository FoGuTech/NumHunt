Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

NumHunt is a number logic puzzle game. This privacy policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

Information We Collect

NumHunt does not collect personal information. The app does not require an account, and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or any other personal data.

Local Game Data

NumHunt saves gameplay history on your device so you can view previously played games. This data is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to us or any third party.

You can clear your local game history from within the app at any time.

Ads and Tracking

NumHunt does not include ads and does not use tracking technologies. The app does not use the device advertising identifier and does not track you across apps or websites.

Analytics and Third-Party Services

NumHunt does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, or any backend services that collect data from the app.

Children's Privacy

NumHunt is designed as a general audience puzzle game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

Data Sharing

Since NumHunt does not collect personal information, we do not sell, rent, or share any personal information with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

If NumHunt's data practices change in the future, this privacy policy will be updated before or when those changes are released.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at fogutech@gmail.com.
